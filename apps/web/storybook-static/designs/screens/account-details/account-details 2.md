# Account details

Single-account details surface (bookings, pending footer, carousel).

## Variants

| Variant | Figma export | Assets |
|---------|--------------|--------|
| `desktop-default` | Account Details - desktop | `variants/desktop-default/default.svg`, `default.png` |
| `mobile-default` | Account Details - mobile | `variants/mobile-default/default.svg`, `default.png` |
