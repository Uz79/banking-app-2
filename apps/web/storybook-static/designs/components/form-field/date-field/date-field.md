# Date field

A form field for entering or selecting a date.

## Variants

- `default`: interactive
- `readonly`: non-editable display (export pending)

## States

Current exports available:

- `default`: `default`, `hover`, `focus`

Planned (export pending):

- `error`, `informative`

## Usage notes

- Prefer a date picker on touch devices and for constrained formats.
- Use **error** only when you can explain the required format/value and provide recovery.
